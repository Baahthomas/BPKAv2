Our Culture

The 8 Hours Culture — eight pillars that define how we live, lead, and love as a Kingdom community.

01 Presence
▾
We are a presence-driven community. Everything we do flows from an encounter with God — worship, prayer, teaching, and service are all expressions of hosting His presence with intentionality and reverence.
02 Prayer
▾
Prayer is not a department — it is our culture. We are a house of prayer that intercedes for individuals, communities, and nations, trusting that Heaven responds to the voice of the righteous.
03 Word
▾
We are rooted in the Word of God — both the written Word (Logos) and the living Word (Rhema). Sound doctrine forms the backbone of our movement, ensuring every believer is grounded and unshakeable.
04 Alignment
▾
Divine alignment is the key to divine assignment. We cultivate a culture of relational and spiritual alignment — to God, to leadership, to community — so that every member walks in maximum Kingdom effectiveness.
05 Honour
▾
Honour is a Kingdom currency. We honour God, leaders, one another, and those we serve. A culture of honour unlocks doors that talent alone cannot open and sustains relationships that carry generational fruit.
06 Activation
▾
We believe every believer is called and gifted. Our culture actively activates dormant gifts, calls, and anointings — moving people from spectators to commissioned carriers of God's power.
07 Assignment
▾
Every person has a God-given assignment. We help believers discover, own, and execute their Kingdom mandate — whether in business, family, ministry, arts, or government. Purpose is not a privilege; it is a birthright.
08 Integrity
▾
Integrity is the foundation of lasting Kingdom impact. We are committed to wholeness — in character, conduct, and covenant — because what we carry must be trusted before it can be transferred.
