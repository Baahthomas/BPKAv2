Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions we receive. Can't find what you need? Send us a message above.

How can I join the Kingdom Alignment Church community?
expand_more
You can join by attending any of our services in person or tuning in online daily at 5PM GMT via PodBean. You can also join our WhatsApp prayer community by clicking the link on our website or contacting us directly. Everyone is welcome — no prior experience or membership required.
Where can I listen to your sermons and podcasts?
expand_more
Our sermons are available on PodBean (live daily at 5PM GMT), Spotify, Apple Music, and all major music and podcast platforms. You can also find select sermons on our website under the "Our Sermons" section.
How do I give or partner with the church?
expand_more
You can give through our website's Give page, or contact us directly via email or WhatsApp for partnership enquiries. All contributions support our ministry, outreach programmes, and the resources we create to equip believers around the world.
Do you offer pastoral counselling or support?
expand_more
Yes. We offer pastoral support to members of our community. Please reach out via the contact form above or send us an email selecting "Pastoral Support" as your subject. All conversations are confidential and handled with care and prayer.
Where can I purchase your books and other resources?
expand_more
Our books and resources are available through our online store on the website. You can browse titles, add them to your cart, and have them delivered. For bulk orders or international shipping enquiries, please contact us directly.
